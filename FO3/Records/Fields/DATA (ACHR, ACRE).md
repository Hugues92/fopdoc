DATA Field
==========

As used in the [ACHR](../ACHR.md) and [ACRE](../ACRE.md) record types.

## Format

Count | Name | Type | Info
------|------|------|-----
- | X | float32 | X position
- | Y | float32 | Y position
- | Z | float32 | Z position
+ | X | float32 | X rotation
+ | Y | float32 | Y rotation
+ | Z | float32 | Z rotation
